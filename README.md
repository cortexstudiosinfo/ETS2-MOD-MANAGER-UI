## ✨ Features

- 🚛 Full support for **Euro Truck Simulator 2** and **American Truck Simulator**.
- 👤 Automatic profile detection.
- 📦 Local and Steam Workshop mod detection.
- 🖼️ Automatic download of official Workshop thumbnails.
- 📑 Easy drag & drop Load Order management.
- ☁️ Cloud Presets to save and restore your mod configurations.
- ⚡ Faster loading and improved overall performance.
- 💥 Built-in crash reporting window with log viewer and contact information.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

### 1. Profile Detection
The application automatically detects your ETS2 and ATS profiles and displays their real names.

### 2. Mod Scanning
It scans both your local `mod` folder and the Steam Workshop, displaying all available mods with their official thumbnails.

### 3. Load Order Management
Reorder your mods easily. The application updates your profile automatically.

### 4. Cloud Presets
Save your Load Order in the cloud and restore it whenever you want, even on another computer.

## ⚙️ Cómo Funciona

### 1. Detección de Perfiles
La aplicación detecta automáticamente tus perfiles de ETS2 y ATS y muestra sus nombres reales.

Busca mods tanto en la carpeta `mod` como en Steam Workshop, mostrando sus miniaturas oficiales.
